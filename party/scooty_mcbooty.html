Scooty McBooty

Origins and Purpose

Scooty spent the first 18 years of her life mastering the Earth element among her people. Shortly after beginning training in Air, she became disgusted by the power-hungry nature of the Savvas race and set out on a search for truth and the path to redemption. During her travels, Scooty has heard many rumors of a legendary holy axe held by a vile necromancer and hopes to earn redemption by killing the necromancer, taking the axe, and laying waste to the undead menace that threatens Gloomhaven.
